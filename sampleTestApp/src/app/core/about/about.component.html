ABOUT US

Revolv and Corvesta Life Insurance Company are among the wholly-owned subsidiaries of Corvesta, Inc. Corvesta also fulfills its mission through a variety of strategic partnerships and investments; including Encara, C3 Jian, Inc. and Healthentic, Inc.
